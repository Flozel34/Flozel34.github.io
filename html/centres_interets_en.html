Judo

I started practicing judo three years ago at the EJM Mauguio Castries club, and it quickly became a major part of my life. I've been able to compete regularly and get involved in volunteering and helping with coaching sessions at the club. Judo has had a strong impact on me both physically and mentally. It has helped me manage my temperament, gain self-confidence, and grow as a person.
